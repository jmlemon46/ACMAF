Current Projects<\/h2>\n
    \n
  • Music Classes for Children with Speech Delay and Special Needs<\/strong> (We are partnered with Marshall Speech and Hearing Center and Birth to Three.)<\/li>\n
  • Virtual Music Classes for Hospitalized Children and Medically Fragile<\/strong> (We are partnered with Shriners Children\u2019s in Dayton and the Ronald McDonald House Charities of Huntington and Dayton)<\/li>\n
  • Huntington Piano Lab<\/strong> (This is a new, upcoming project in which children who are underserved in the community will be taught how to read music and play the piano for one year.)<\/li>\n
  • General Scholarships for the Arts<\/strong> (We support music, art, dance, and acting classes, etc.)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
